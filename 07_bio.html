Born 1996 in Heilongjiang, China, Jiuxin Zhu grew up in Beijing and received his Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018 and his Master of Science in Integrated Digital Media at New York University in 2020. Based in Jersey City, NJ, he volunteers for Nonviolence International New York as a videographer while being a freelance video editor and web developer. He is open to various jobs in producing, film shooting and post-production.
