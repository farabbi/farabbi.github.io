Born 1996 in Heilongjiang, China, Jiuxin Zhu grew up in Beijing and received his Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018 and his Master of Science in Integrated Digital Media at New York University in 2020. He currently focuses on filmmaking, motion capture and virtual production and is based in Brooklyn, NY. He makes documentaries, live actions and animations with Adobe Premiere and Unreal Engine. He does photography and short videos in his spare time and has a consistent love in sports. He is now looking for jobs and is open to freelance works.
